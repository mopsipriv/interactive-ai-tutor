January - Spring Semester Start
The spring semester begins in January. Tutors should organize orientation meetings 
with new students. Check that all students have registered for correct courses.
Remind students about study right deadlines. First month is critical for identifying 
students who may need extra support.

February - Mid-Winter Check
February is time for mid-winter progress review. Check student credit accumulation.
Students who are behind schedule should be contacted individually. Verify that 
study rights are valid until end of semester...

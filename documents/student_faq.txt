Q: What is study right and how long do I have?
A: Study right (opiskeluoikeus) determines how long you can study at the university.
For bachelor's degree programs it is typically 4-5 years from enrollment date.
Check your valid_until date in the system.

Q: How many credits do I need per semester?
A: To stay on track, you should complete approximately 30 credits per academic year,
or about 15 credits per semester.

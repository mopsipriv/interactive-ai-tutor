TVT Program - Tietotekniikka Curriculum Guide

Semester 1 - Foundation
Students begin with mathematical foundations and introduction to programming.
Matematiikan perusteet provides necessary mathematical background.
Johdatus ohjelmointiin is the gateway course for all programming studies.
Completing semester 1 courses is mandatory before advancing to semester 2.

Semester 2 - Core Programming
Ohjelmointi 1 builds on introduction course. Tietokannat teaches database fundamentals.

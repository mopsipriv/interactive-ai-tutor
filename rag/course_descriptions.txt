TVT1003 - Johdatus ohjelmointiin (Introduction to Programming)
This is the foundational programming course. Students learn basic programming 
concepts using C. This course is prerequisite for all advanced programming 
courses. Students who struggle here should seek extra tutoring immediately.
Recommended semester: 1. Credits: 5. Category: mandatory.

TVT1006 - Tietokannat (Databases)
Students learn relational database design and SQL. This course is required 
for AI Chatbot Development project. Prerequisites: TVT1003.
Recommended semester: 2. Credits: 5. Category: mandatory.

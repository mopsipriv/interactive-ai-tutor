Study Right (Opiskeluoikeus) Guide

What is study right?
Study right determines how long a student can study at the university.
For bachelor's degree (AMK) it is typically 3-4 years from enrollment date.

What happens when study right expires?
If study right expires, student cannot attend courses or exams.
Student must apply for extension through the student office.

Warning signs - contact student if:
- Less than 6 months remaining on study right
- Credits earned are below 50% of expected credits
- Student has not completed any courses in current semester
